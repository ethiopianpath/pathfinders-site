About Pathfinders

Our Aim

The Pathfinder Club is part of a global program designed for young people to develop Christ-like characters, cultivate leadership skills, and serve their communities through various activities that promote physical, mental, and spiritual growth.

The Pathfinder Law

  1. Keep the morning watch
  2. Do my honest part
  3. Care for my body
  4. keep a level eye
  5. Be courteous and obedient
  6. Walk softly in the sanctuary
  7. Keep a song in my heart
  8. Go on God's errands

The Pathfinder Pledge

"By the grace of God,

I will be pure, kind, and true.

I will keep the Pathfinder Law.

I will be a servant of God

and a friend to man."

Frequently Asked Questions

Our Pathfinder Club is open to youth ages 10-15. We have different classes and activities tailored to different age groups within this range.
For regular meetings: Bible, notebook, pen, and water bottle. For outdoor activities: appropriate clothing, sturdy shoes, and any specific items listed in our weekly announcements.
Annual membership fee is 500 ETB which includes the Pathfinder handbook and basic supplies. Some special activities may have additional costs, but we offer scholarships for families in need.
While not required, parent volunteers are always welcome! We especially need help with transportation, event planning, and skill instruction. All volunteers must complete a background check.
We incorporate Amharic in our activities, and study Ethiopian Christian history. Many of our service projects also focus on local community needs.
